# Chocolat mousse

## Ingredients

- **200 g** dark chocolate
- **3** eggs (separated)
- **1 tbsp** sugar
- **Pinch** of salt

## Instructions

- Melt chocolate, let cool slightly.
- Whisk yolks into chocolate.
- Beat whites with salt and sugar until stiff.
- Fold whites into chocolate gently.
- Chill 2+ hours.
